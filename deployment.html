Self-Hosted Metacloud

For regulated organisations.

Portal, Electros, C4 and connectors run inside the customer boundary.

  • Maximum sovereignty

Federated Metacloud

For providers and sovereign federations.

Multiple providers participate in a common federation while preserving operational independence.

  • Operational independence
  • Shared interoperability
  • No central authority
  • Peer-to-peer federation

Federated Provider Deployment

Ideal for neoclouds, sovereign providers and service providers who need to broker third-party infrastructure under their own governance model.
