What Atomosquare Is

Atomosquare is the distributed hypervisor connectivity layer of the Elemento platform.

It consists of hypervisor-side connectors and federation endpoints that expose virtualization platforms and private cloud infrastructures to the Metacloud through a common interoperability model.

Rather than replacing existing virtualization environments, Atomosquare enables heterogeneous hypervisors to participate in a common federation while preserving operational independence.

Atomosquare is not itself a hypervisor — it is the connectivity fabric that joins hypervisors to Electros.

Why It Matters

Atomosquare prevents VMware exit from becoming another lock-in cycle.

Instead of forcing a one-time migration into a new hypervisor silo, Atomosquare allows legacy and new environments to be governed under the same control plane.

Migration becomes incremental, governed and reversible.

AtomOS Inside Atomosquare

AtomOS is Elemento's native sovereign virtualization endpoint inside Atomosquare.

VMware, Proxmox, Nutanix, Hyper-V and OpenStack are third-party endpoints inside the same fabric.

Electros governs both native and third-party environments through the same operational model.
