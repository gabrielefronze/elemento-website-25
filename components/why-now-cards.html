Infrastructure Dependency Crisis

The Broadcom acquisition exposed the cost and risk of single-vendor infrastructure dependency. Organisations own workloads, but not always their freedom to move them.

Digital Sovereignty Imperative

NIS2, DORA, the EU Data Act and the EU AI Act are making infrastructure jurisdiction, data residency and operational control board-level concerns.

AI Infrastructure Fragmentation

AI workloads require access to GPUs, specialised infrastructure, sovereign environments and multiple providers. No single provider delivers it all.
